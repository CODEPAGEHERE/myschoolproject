MyExamSpace

Streamlining Exam Management for Nigerian Schools

MyExamSpace is a robust computer-based testing (CBT) solution tailored for schools in Nigeria. It enables seamless creation, administration, and grading of exams across various classes and subjects, both online and offline.

Key Features

  • Question Bank: Create and manage reusable exam questions.
  • Auto-Grading: Instantly grades objective questions and provides analytics.
  • Student Profiles: Assign tests by class or individual student.
  • Secure Login: Students and teachers log in with unique access codes.
  • Offline Mode: Works without internet during exams.

Perfect For

  • Primary and secondary schools
  • Exam coordinators and school admins
  • Education startups building school systems
